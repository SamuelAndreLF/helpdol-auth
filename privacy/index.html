HelpDol — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

HelpDol is a financial management app for drivers, self-employed workers, and small businesses. This policy explains what data we collect, why we use it, who we share it with, how long we keep it, and how you can control it. It covers the HelpDol app, the ChatGPT integration (ChatGPT App), and the bank connection.

1. Who is responsible

HelpDol is the controller of your data. For any privacy matter, or to export or delete your data, contact us at helpdolsuport@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

CategoryWhat it is
AccountYour email and account authentication data.
SubscriptionYour plan and subscription status (basic, pro, premium).
Financial recordsIncome, expenses, payments to collaborators, scheduled transactions, goals, and categories you create in the app or through ChatGPT.
Receipt imagesPhotos of receipts/invoices you upload for automatic reading (OCR).
Voice commandsAudio you record to add entries by voice, converted to text.
Bank transactionsWhen you connect your bank, we import only your transaction history (amount, date, description, and merchant). We never see or store your bank password or credentials — the bank login is handled by Stripe. The connection is read-only; HelpDol does not move money.
Phone number (optional)If you use HelpDol over WhatsApp/SMS, we store the number to link messages to your account.
Audit logsA record of actions performed (including via ChatGPT) for security and history.

3. How we use your data (purposes)

  • Show your history, balances, summaries, and financial projections.
  • Perform the actions you request (add income/expense, generate a receipt, generate a report, schedule a payment).
  • Automatically import and reconcile transactions from the bank you connected.
  • Generate answers and insights from the AI assistant (in the app and in ChatGPT).
  • Manage your subscription and unlock plan features.
  • Ensure security, prevent fraud, and keep an audit trail.

We do not use your financial data for advertising, and we do not sell your data.

4. ChatGPT integration — inputs and outputs

When you connect your HelpDol account to ChatGPT (authorization via OAuth 2.1, which you can revoke at any time), ChatGPT can call HelpDol's tools on your behalf. Below is what each type of tool receives and returns:

ToolInputOutput
Record income / expense / payment to collaboratorAmount, description, category, date, and currency you provide.Confirmation of the created entry.
Get financial summary / trendThe requested period.Totals for income, expenses, balance, and projection.
List / detail / classify transactionsPeriod, transaction id, chosen classification.A list of entries (from the app and the bank), with amount, date, description, and source.
Generate receipt / report / accountant historyA period or a specific entry.A document or link with the summary/history for the period.
Query collaborators, goals, accounts payable/receivablePeriod/filter.The corresponding data from your account.

HelpDol receives from ChatGPT only the commands needed to perform the action. The content of your ChatGPT conversations is handled by OpenAI under its own privacy policy. Every action taken via ChatGPT is recorded in your audit log.

5. Who we share with (recipients)

We share data only with service providers needed to operate HelpDol, each handling only the data indicated:

ProviderPurpose

OpenAIProcessing of the AI assistant and the ChatGPT integration.
StripeBank connection (Financial Connections): performs the login to your bank and sends us the transaction history. Stripe handles your banking credentials.
RevenueCatSubscription and plan management.
TwilioSending/receiving messages when you use HelpDol over WhatsApp/SMS.
OCR and voice providersReading receipts and transcribing voice commands.

We may also disclose data if required by law or a court order.

6. Retention — how long we keep your data

  • Your financial records and account data are kept while your account is active.
  • When you delete your account, your personal and financial data are erased from our systems, except what we must retain by law (e.g., tax/accounting records).
  • Audit logs are kept for up to 12 months for security, then removed.
  • When you disconnect your bank, importing of new transactions stops immediately. Transactions already imported remain in your history until you delete them or delete your account.
  • Access tokens (including ChatGPT's) expire automatically and are only refreshed while the authorization is active.

7. Security

All data travels over HTTPS and is protected by authentication. Each user can only access their own data (per-account isolation). We do not receive or store bank passwords. Access tokens expire on their own.

8. Your rights and controls

  • Export your data (CSV) from the app's Settings.
  • Delete your account and your data from the app's Settings.
  • Revoke ChatGPT's access at any time (in the authorization/connector).
  • Disconnect your bank at any time on the bank connection screen.
  • Request access, correction, or deletion by writing to helpdolsuport@gmail.com.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as HelpDol evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates the current version. Significant changes will be communicated in the app.
